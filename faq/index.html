Frequently Asked Questions

This page answers common questions about MasonicWorld.com, the resources available on the site, and how the materials may be used.

What is MasonicWorld.com?

MasonicWorld.com is a collection of Masonic resources, educational materials, graphics, tracing boards, forms, certificates, and related reference materials created to assist Freemasons, lodges, and researchers.

Are the resources free?

Yes. During the current beta relaunch, all resources are available free of charge.

Do I need to register or create an account?

No. Resources may be viewed or downloaded without creating an account.

Can I use these resources in my lodge?

In most cases, yes. Resources are provided for personal, educational, and lodge use. Please review the Legal page for additional information regarding copyright and permitted use.

Can I share resources from MasonicWorld.com?

You may share links to resources and pages on MasonicWorld.com. Please refer others to the website rather than redistributing files whenever possible.

Why can't I download the articles?

Many articles are displayed online only. Download permissions vary depending on the original author and source material.

What are Tracing Boards?

Tracing Boards are visual teaching aids used in Masonic instruction. The site includes a variety of tracing board presentations and educational materials covering different degrees and topics.

What are Awards, Certificates, and Records?

This section contains lodge forms, certificates, awards, record-keeping documents, and related administrative materials designed for lodge use.

I found a broken link or error. How can I report it?

Please use the Contact page to report broken links, missing files, or other issues. Feedback helps improve the site for everyone.

How often is the site updated?

MasonicWorld.com is updated on an ongoing basis. New resources, corrections, and improvements may be added at any time.

Can I submit content?

Possibly. If you have material that may be useful to the Masonic community, please contact us with details about the resource.

Does MasonicWorld.com represent a Grand Lodge?

No. MasonicWorld.com is an independent resource website and is not the official website of any Grand Lodge or Masonic governing body.

How long has MasonicWorld.com been online?

MasonicWorld.com was originally launched in the late 1990s and has served the Masonic community for decades. Additional information is available on the Site History page.

Why was the site redesigned?

The site was rebuilt to improve organization, simplify maintenance, enhance mobile compatibility, and make resources easier to locate and access.

Will more resources be added?

Yes. The site will continue to grow as additional resources are reviewed, updated, and added over time.
