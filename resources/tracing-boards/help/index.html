Using the Tracing Boards

The tracing boards are provided in Microsoft PowerPoint format. Most users can view and edit the presentations using Microsoft PowerPoint.

If you do not have Microsoft PowerPoint, several free alternatives are available, including LibreOffice Impress and Google Slides.

The presentations may be customized to meet the needs of your lodge. You may add, remove, or rearrange slides, insert your own lodge information, and adjust colors or formatting as desired.

For best results, review the presentation before use and make any modifications needed for your audience and jurisdiction.
