Tracing Boards

The Masonic Tracing Boards were developed to help move lodge instruction into digital presentations for the Entered Apprentice, Fellow Craft, and Master Mason degrees.

The collection included tracing boards for EA, FC, and MM, along with breakdowns of each section. This provided multiple ways to teach students and explain the symbolism of the degrees.

Included Resource Areas

  • PowerPoint presentations in three categories: tracing boards (12 slides), degree sections (40 slides), and color sections (63 slides).
  • Supporting material in the form of eBooks and reference materials are also included at the bottom of this page.
