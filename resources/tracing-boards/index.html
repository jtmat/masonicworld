Tracing Boards

The Masonic Tracing Board resources were developed to help move lodge instruction into digital presentations for the Entered Apprentice, Fellow Craft, and Master Mason degrees.

The collection included tracing boards for EA, FC, and MM, along with breakdowns of each section. This provided multiple ways to teach students and explain the symbolism of the degrees.

Supporting material was also included so users can build text, slide notes, or written explanations to accompany the tracing board presentations.

Benefits

  • Useful for training Entered Apprentices, Fellow Crafts, and Master Masons
  • Designed for laptop-based lodge instruction and presentations
  • Helpful for explaining Masonic symbolism and degree work
  • Material could be added, removed, or adapted for local use

Slides

The Tracing Board collection included 115 Microsoft PowerPoint slides in three categories:

  • Tracing Boards - 12 slides, including black and blue versions
  • EA, FC, and MM sections - 40 slides, including black and blue versions
  • EA, FC, and MM color sections - 63 color slides
