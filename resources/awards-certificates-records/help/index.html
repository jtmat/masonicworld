Using Awards, Certificates & Records

The Awards, Certificates & Records collection includes Microsoft Word templates, boiler text, degree work forms, record pages, and related materials for lodge and personal use.

Most files are provided in Word format so they can be opened, edited, copied, pasted, and customized as needed. You may change names, dates, lodge information, wording, colors, and layout to fit your purpose.

Templates and Boiler Text

Templates provide the basic design or layout. Boiler text provides sample wording that may be copied into a template or edited for your own award, certificate, record, or presentation.

Printing

For best results, review the document before printing and test your printer settings. Heavier paper or certificate paper may improve the final appearance.

Supporting Files

Additional boiler text, degree work boiler text, and template color references are available from the main Awards, Certificates & Records page.
