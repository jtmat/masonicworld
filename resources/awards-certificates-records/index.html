Awards, Certificates & Records

Award, Certificate, and Record was originally developed as a collection of Masonic templates, certificates, records, degree work forms, and related resources for lodges and Masonic officers.

This section is being preserved and reorganized as part of the MasonicWorld.com rebuild. The original material included awards and certificates, Masonic records, degree work forms for EA, FC, MM, AASR, and York Rite, Masonic graphics, and Masonic fonts.

These resources can be useful for lodge awards nights, candidate records, officer use, lodge presentations, and historical reference.

Included Resource Areas

  • Awards, certificates, and records

Award Boiler Text

The collection includeds boiler text for common award and recognition categories.

  • Training
