Masonic Graphics

The graphics are a set of graphics that can be colored, resized, and changed. They were designed so users could build graphics for lodge correspondence, lodge websites, presentations, shirts, and other projects.

The original collection included instructions and an RGB color list to help users choose colors for their projects.

The premium graphics collection included more than 310 graphics in four sections:

  • Blue Lodge - graphics for EA, FC, and MM
  • Square and Compasses - EA, FC, MM, and Past Master
  • Eastern Star - graphics for Eastern Star seats
  • Other Houses - Scottish Rite and York Rite graphics
