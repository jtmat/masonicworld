WHAT EACH MASON SHOULD READ

There are a number of subjects not addressed at this website but are historically located and found in other Masonic publications. It is recommended that each Master Mason have at a minimum for his personal use the following books (please notice the word minimum):

Masonic Ritual

(Your State or G. L.) Monitor and Freemason's Guide to the Symbolic Degrees

Your Lodge By Laws

Roberts Rules of Order

Mackey's Jurisprudence of Freemasonry

Masonic Funeral Services with Instructions
