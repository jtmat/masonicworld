About MasonicWorld.com

MasonicWorld.com is being rebuilt as a central home for Masonic articles, resources, downloads, graphics, and legacy Masonic software material.

The site brings together material that has been developed, collected, and shared over many years through MasonicWorld.com, MasonicSoftware.com, and related Masonic resource projects.

What’s New

  • June 2026: Rebuilding MasonicWorld.com as a consolidated Masonic resource site.
  • June 2026: Articles, eBooks, graphics, tracing boards, and other legacy resources are being reorganized.

Legacy Product History

  • The MasonicCD™ debuted as a Masonic Graphic CD in 1997 out of London, England, United Kingdom.
  • Late 1999 the first MasonicCD featuring screensavers and educational tools was created.
  • Early 2001 MasonicCD Version 6 was introduced.
  • Late 2001 five new products arrived: MCD Premium, Test, Professional, Standard, and Music.
  • Middle 2002 an additional two products, MCD Ultimate and Library, were added to the product line.
  • Dec 2002 updates across the entire line of products.
  • June 2003 Masonic Database introduced.
  • July 2003 Ultimate Plus introduced.
  • July 2003 Masonic Music Plus introduced.
  • August 2003 Masonic Board Game introduced.
  • Sep 2003 Masonic Classification System introduced.
  • Oct 2003 Masonic Web Hosting introduced.
  • Nov 2003 Masonic Tracing Board introduced.
  • May 2004 update of the MasonicCD product line.
  • July 2004 update to Assistant Secretary database.
  • May 2005 update to Assistant Secretary database.
  • August 2005 start to phase out the “MasonicCD” name.
  • September 2005 update to Mosaic product line.
  • November 12, 2005 launch of Masonic Software Tracing Board.
  • March 10, 2008 launch of Award, Certificate, and Record.
  • March 10, 2008 upgrades to Mosaic line from 7.0 to 8.0.
  • March 10, 2008 upgrades to Tracing Board from 2.0 to 3.0.
  • March 10, 2008 upgrades to Music Plus from 2.0 to 3.0.
  • April 2010 update to Assistant Secretary database.
  • October 2020 Assistant Secretary 5.0 made available for free.

As MasonicWorld.com is rebuilt, many of these older resources will be reorganized, preserved, and made easier to access.
