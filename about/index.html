About

Founded in 1997, MasonicWorld.com began as an effort to provide Freemasons with educational resources, graphics, software, and reference materials that could be used by individual members and lodges alike.

The site's founder believed that Masonic education should be readily available to new Freemasons, lodge officers, and those seeking a deeper understanding of the Craft. That philosophy continues to guide the site today.

MasonicWorld.com now brings together articles, eBooks, graphics, tracing boards, awards, certificates, records, and other resources collected and developed over nearly three decades of service to the Masonic community.
