Site History

  • 1997 – The MasonicCD™ debuted as a Masonic graphics CD in London, England.
  • Late 1999 – The first MasonicCD featuring screensavers and educational tools was released.
  • Early 2001 – MasonicCD Version 6 was introduced.
  • Late 2001 – Five new products arrived: MCD Premium, Test, Professional, Standard, and Music.
  • Mid 2002 – Two additional products, MCD Ultimate and Library, were added to the product line.
  • December 2002 – Updates were released across the entire product line.
  • June 2003 – Masonic Database was introduced.
  • July 2003 – Ultimate Plus was introduced.
  • July 2003 – Masonic Music Plus was introduced.
  • August 2003 – Masonic Board Game was introduced.
  • September 2003 – Masonic Classification System was introduced.
  • October 2003 – Masonic Web Hosting was introduced.
  • November 2003 – Masonic Tracing Board was introduced.
  • May 2004 – The MasonicCD product line was updated.
  • July 2004 – Assistant Secretary database was updated.
  • May 2005 – Assistant Secretary database was updated.
  • August 2005 – The MasonicCD name began to be phased out.
  • September 2005 – The Mosaic product line was updated.
  • November 12, 2005 – Masonic Software Tracing Board was launched.
  • March 10, 2008 – Award, Certificate, and Record was launched.
  • March 10, 2008 – Mosaic was upgraded from Version 7.0 to Version 8.0.
  • March 10, 2008 – Tracing Board was upgraded from Version 2.0 to Version 3.0.
  • March 10, 2008 – Music Plus was upgraded from Version 2.0 to Version 3.0.
  • April 2010 – Assistant Secretary database was updated.
  • October 2020 – Assistant Secretary 5.0 was made available free of charge.
  • July 2026 – MasonicWorld.com and MasonicSoftware.com were combined into a single Masonic resource website, bringing together decades of articles, eBooks, graphics, tracing boards, forms, and educational materials under one location.
